About Urban Help

Dedicated to providing exceptional cleaning and care services with professionalism, reliability, and attention to detail.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Urban Help began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, reliable cleaning services that make a difference in people's lives. What started as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into a trusted network of experienced cleaners, caregivers, and household helpers.

Our founder, Priya Sharma, recognized the need for a service that not only cleaned homes but also provided peace of mind. After experiencing firsthand the challenges of finding trustworthy household help, she decided to create a solution that would benefit both clients and service professionals.

Today, Urban Help serves thousands of homes and businesses across India, maintaining the same commitment to excellence and integrity that has defined us from the beginning.
